Who is Artsybasy?

Bassey Daniel Nyong
a multifaceted creative force from Uruan LGA in Akwa-Ibom state, Nigeria, boasts a rich tapestry of achievements.
With prowess in sculpture, painting, and art education, Nyong has garnered acclaim both locally and internationally.
His dedication to nurturing budding talent and mentoring others is underscored by his numerous accolades and awards.
As a champion of creativity, Bassey continues to inspire and uplift through his artistry and philanthropy.
